From International Workshop on Digital Forensics in the Cloud (IWDFC 2012):

"The 2012 workshop will comprise of presentations from a cloud forensic expert from industry, and a representative from one of the popular cloud platform developers (e.g. Nimbula director).  A lab session will also be carried out on setting up and management of a cloud infrastructure."


The work will run in conjunction with the 2012 ISSA conference ( http://www.infosecsa.co.za/) and will take place on the third day (17th August 2012).

Submissions are welcome for people who wish to present at the workshop.

Topics of interest must be based on the cloud and are but not limited to:

·         Cloud security
·         Digital forensic tools
·         Incident response
·         Data visualization
·         Digital evidence identification
·         Digital forensic process
·         Privacy
