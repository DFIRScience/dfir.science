--- layout: post title: International Symposium on Cybercrime Response (ISCR) 2013 date: '2013-06-26T15:31:00.002+09:00' tags: modified_time: '2013-06-26T15:31:54.875+09:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2701259639305045003.post-5110444623351889429 blogger_orig_url: http://www.cybercrimetech.com/2013/06/international-symposium-on-cybercrime.html --- The International Symposium on Cybercrime Response (ISCR) 2013 held in Jung-gu, Seoul, South Korea on the 25th to the 27th of June.

The first day covered mobile device analysis.

On the second day, keynote speaker, Kim Sang-hun CEO of NHN, discussed enhancing cooperation between online service providers and law-enforcement agencies in the Korean setting.
