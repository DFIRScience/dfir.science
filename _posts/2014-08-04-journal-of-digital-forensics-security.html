If you have never seen the JDFSL before, now is a great time to check out the current issue as well as all the new features.

We purposefully chose to use the word cyber in our tagline, instead of digital to emphasize the cyber culture surrounding computing, and the word cyber also extends itself beyond the technical domain of computing.
